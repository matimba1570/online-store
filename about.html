Corporate Profile

Eswatini's Supplier of Choice

We help the growth and development trajectory of Eswatini's technology sector in response to a changing world.

The purpose of ESWATINI TECHNOCRATS CENTER is to modernize computer electronics service. Elevate the bar for ICT electronics design and execution in Eswatini by taking the lead in private sector innovation. Our goal is to establish a name for excellence and innovation that will allow us to provide our service across the nation.

Our Vision

To establish a name for excellence and innovation that will allow us to provide our service across the nation. We exhibit proficiency in network designs, point of sale systems, hardware maintenance for information technology, and internship practice in order to provide needed ICT solutions.

Our Mission

To be the best in offering our cherished customers effective computer electronics solutions. We accomplish this by fusing cutting-edge hardware, solid network infrastructure, and modern software technology with the ingenuity of exceptionally talented individuals with vast combined expertise.
