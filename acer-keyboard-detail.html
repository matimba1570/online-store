Specifications

  • Keyboard Type: Full-size laptop keyboard with numeric keypad
  • Switch Type: Membrane switches with tactile feedback
  • Key Travel: 1.5mm actuation distance for comfortable typing
  • Backlighting: LED backlighting (RGB options available for gaming models)
  • Lifecycle: 10 million keystrokes rated durability
  • Interface: Integrated keyboard controller with laptop motherboard
  • Operating Temperature: 0°C to 40°C (standard operating range)

All Acer keyboard assemblies include the keyboard, backlight cable (if applicable), and mounting hardware. Keys feature laser-etched legends that resist fading over time. Gaming models include anti-ghosting technology and dedicated macro keys for enhanced performance.

Compatibility

These Acer keyboard assemblies are designed specifically for the following laptop models:

  • Acer Aspire 3/5/7 series (standard consumer laptops)
  • Acer Nitro 5/7 and Predator Helios series (gaming laptops with RGB lighting)
  • Acer Swift 3/X series (ultrabooks with backlit options)
  • Acer TravelMate and Extensa series (business laptops)
  • Acer Spin 5/7 series (convertible laptops with spill-resistant design)
  • Acer ConceptD series (creative workstations with precision keys)
  • Acer Chromebook 514 (cloud-based devices)

Before purchasing, verify your laptop model number and ensure the keyboard layout matches your regional requirements (QWERTY, QWERTZ, AZERTY, etc.).

Warranty

Your purchase includes:

  • 1-year limited manufacturer warranty for defects in materials and workmanship
  • 30-day replacement guarantee on DOA (dead on arrival) products
  • Lifetime technical support via email and chat for installation questions

Warranty coverage does not include physical damage from drops, liquid spills, or user misuse. Professional installation is recommended to maintain warranty validity.
