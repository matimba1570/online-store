Specifications

  • Memory Type: DDR4 SODIMM (260-pin)
  • Speed: 2133MHz to 3600MHz options (varies by module)
  • Capacity per module: 4GB, 8GB, 16GB, 32GB
  • Voltage: 1.2V (standard low-voltage DDR4) and 1.35V for performance variants
  • CAS Latency: CL15 - CL22, optimized for balance of responsiveness and stability
  • Operating Temperature: 0°C to 85°C (Acer Enduro rugged options certified for harsh conditions)
  • Form Factor: 260-pin SODIMM designed for laptops and small form factor systems

All Acer RAM modules are built for easy upgrade: single-module and dual-channel modes are supported based on your laptop model, helping increase multitasking performance, reduce lag, and improve CPU/GPU throughput during graphics tasks.

Compatibility

These Acer RAM modules are matched to brand lines and verified for BIOS compatibility across current and legacy Acer systems:

  • Acer Aspire 3/5/7 series (general consumer laptops)
  • Acer Nitro 5/7 and Predator Helios series (gaming)
  • Acer Swift series (thin-and-light ultrabooks)
  • Acer TravelMate and Extensa series (business & budget)
  • Acer Spin 5/7 (convertibles with hybrid mode)
  • Acer ConceptD and Enduro (creator & rugged workstations)

Before adding RAM, verify your laptop’s maximum memory configuration in Acer support docs and check whether one or two slots are available for the upgrade.

Warranty

Your purchase includes:

  • 1-year limited manufacturer warranty for defects in materials and workmanship
  • 30-day replacement guarantee on DOA (dead on arrival) products
  • Lifetime technical support via email and chat for installation questions

Warranty coverage does not include physical damage, liquid spills, or user misuse. Register your product on the manufacturer’s site for expedited service.
