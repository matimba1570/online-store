Biometric Entry

Biometric entry systems use unique physical characteristics, such as fingerprints, facial recognition, or iris scans, to grant access to secured areas. These systems offer high security by ensuring only authorized individuals can enter.

Advantages include reduced risk of lost keys or stolen cards, quick verification, and integration with other security systems.
