Next-Gen Firewalls

Next-generation firewalls (NGFW) provide advanced threat protection beyond traditional firewalls. They integrate deep packet inspection, intrusion prevention, and application awareness to defend against sophisticated cyber attacks.

These firewalls use AI and machine learning to identify and block emerging threats in real-time.

Key Features

  • Advanced Threat Detection
  • Deep Packet Inspection
  • AI-Powered Analysis
  • SSL Decryption
