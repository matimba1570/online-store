Multi-Factor Authentication

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) adds extra layers of security by requiring multiple verification methods. It combines something you know (password), something you have (phone), and something you are (biometric).

MFA significantly reduces the risk of unauthorized access, even if passwords are compromised.

Key Features

  • SMS & App-Based Codes
  • Biometric Verification
  • Hardware Tokens
  • Phishing Protection
