Role-Based Access

Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) assigns permissions based on user roles within an organization. It ensures users have access only to the resources necessary for their job functions.

This model simplifies administration and reduces the risk of unauthorized access by enforcing the principle of least privilege.

Key Features

  • Role Assignment
  • Least Privilege
  • Centralized Management
  • Compliance Support
